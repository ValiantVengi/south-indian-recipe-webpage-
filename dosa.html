Dosa Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup rice
  • ½ cup urad dal
  • ½ tsp fenugreek seeds
  • Water
  • Salt to taste

Instructions:

  1. Soak rice and urad dal for 4-6 hours.
  2. Grind the mixture to a smooth batter and ferment overnight.
  3. Heat a pan, pour the batter, and spread it into a thin circle.
  4. Cook until golden brown and serve with chutney and sambar.
