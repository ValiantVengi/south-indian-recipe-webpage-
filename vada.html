Vada Recipe

Ingredients:
  • 1 cup urad dal (split black gram)
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • Green chilies, chopped
  • Curry leaves
  • Salt to taste

Instructions:

  1. Soak urad dal for 4-6 hours and grind it into a smooth batter.
  2. Add chopped onions, green chilies, curry leaves, and salt to the batter.
  3. Shape the batter into doughnuts and fry them in hot oil until golden brown.
  4. Serve with chutney and sambar.
