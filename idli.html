Idli Recipe

Ingredients:
  • 2 cups rice
  • 1 cup urad dal
  • Â½ tsp fenugreek seeds
  • Salt

Instructions:

  1. Soak rice and urad dal for 4-6 hours.
  2. Grind the mixture into a smooth batter and let it ferment overnight.
  3. Grease idli molds, pour batter into molds, and steam for 10-12 minutes.
  4. Serve with coconut chutney and sambar.
